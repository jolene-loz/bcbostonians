About Us

The Bostonians of Boston College is BC's original and premiere a cappella group. For THIRTY crazy years we've been making music, traveling from coast to coast, and having a whole lot of fun along the way.

And our accomplishments

We've traveled to California, Ireland, Montreal, Virginia, Washington DC, New York City, Disneyland, the Hamptons, Quebec, and all over New England.

We've appeared on national television on CBS's "The Price is Right," on MTV's "The Real World" and on NBC's "Sunday Night Football."

Our members/alumni have been contestants on American Idol, featured on the Today Show, and even have their own hit songs on Spotify!

And we have over 7000+ listeners each month on Spotify
